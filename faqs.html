FAQ Web Design

  • How long will it take for my website to be designed?

    Well, it depends on the size of your website content. If you want a single page website with less content then it can take approximately 24hrs to 48hrs maximum. For holistically large content or multi-paged websites we will obviously take longer but the most possible minimum time to design your website based on your requirements.

  • Do I necessarily have to schedule a meeting with you guys to discuss the requirements for my website's design?

    Not necessarily, if we are within your reach then you can else you can use any digital means at your disposal to effectively communicate with us. You can send us an email providing all your requirements and the resources(such as content) we may need to assist you.

  • Once my website design is completed, what after-sales service do you offer?

    We fully support our clients incase they need anything regarding the website we designed for them. Be it maintenance or any difficulty or clarity concerning hosting of the website. We regard any business transaction as a relationship worth investing upon even after serving you with our top class services, as a result we are always willing to assist our clients henceforth.

  • Do you provide hosting services as well?

    Yes, we can host your website in our platforms if you allow us to do so. We have a wide range of hosting packages that you can choose from to suit your business needs. Check out our services for more details.

  • Do i necessarilly have to host my website in your platforms? Can i move my website to another hosting platform besides you even if you are the one who initially hosted it?

    Its not compulsory for you to use our hosting services. We design the website for you then once its completed we let you decide whether to keep your website on our platforms or to host it anywhere else. For instance, if you already have a domain name and a platform you can host it there or just any other company of your choice which offers hosting services. You can definitely move your website form our platform anytime you want, all it takes is your word then "ROGER THAT!" will be our response.

  • Will my website be search engine optimized?

    All our websites are designed with search engines in mind to ensure that there can be tractiokn to your website through searches from browser.Brandsaints offers SEO (Search Engine Optimization) as one of the exclusive services included in our packages that wil surely help boost traffic to your website. Check out our services to meticulously learn about SEOs.

FAQ Graphic Design

  • Do i own my designs?

    Yes, once the design is completed then its totally yours because you parchased it from us. However we as Brandsaints have a contractual agreement with our clients that we retain rights to use your design in our portfolio to advertise our work or to prove our worth to investors. Once we are done with your design we will hand over the design to you in the appropriate and prefered format so you can immediately show off your brand.

  • When do you need your payment? Before or after the job is complete?

    Well, that one depends. You can either decide to pay once off or before the job or just choose to pay 50% deposit then we start working on your designs. We do not accept payments after the job except on a signed legal contract, reasons being that we want to avoid situations whereby people will be leaving their designs on us unpaid for while we invested all our valuable time and resources doing those designs.

  • How long does the enire design process take? eg, to design a logo.

    Depends on what we are designing, for logo and letterhead it can just be 24hrs to 48hrs depending on the effectiveness of our communication with our client because it takes two to tango. If you dont give us the right response at the right time when we need it then its obviously going to take time. For other designs like flyers, brochures, billboards e.t.c we take 5 days on average depending on our workload, 7 days maximum.

  • Can i copy a design i saw form the internet or just somewhere?

    As professional graphic designers, we at Brandsaints believe in creating designs that will make you stand out among the rest of your competitors, so we cannot copy a design that has already been made by another graphic designer. We take other designers' work as motivation and inspiration to be more creative and come up with unique designs that will inspire the upcoming ones too.

  • Is there an extra fee for rush designs?

    Yes, if you really wish to expedit your designs then you will be charged 30% extra on the original price and your design will be completed within 24hrs on average depending on your content.

  • Do you do consultations? are there referal benefits?

    Yes, we do consultation for our clients so we can be able to identify and address their needs. After the consultation we give them an invoice or quotation regarding our services. We offer referal benefits also, any client who refers people to us will get the chance to enjoy discount prices on future work or on any service offered by brandsaints.
